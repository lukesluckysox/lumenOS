The Architecture

Four instruments.
One epistemic loop.

Each tool operates independently, but their power is in sequence. Together they close a system of reflective inquiry — one that spirals toward earned understanding, not comfortable answers.

01

Liminal

Questions belief

The threshold instrument. Liminal surfaces assumptions you did not know you held — the inherited frameworks, silent priors, and borrowed certainties that quietly shape every judgment. Where inquiry begins and nothing is taken as given.

02

Parallax

Reveals pattern

The pattern instrument. Parallax reads across time — your writing, responses, and choices — to surface recurring structures in thought, emotion, and behavior. What persists beneath the noise is what matters. What repeats is what you are.

In development Enter

03

Praxis

Tests hypothesis

The experimental instrument. Praxis converts insight into lived experiment. If Liminal surfaces the question and Parallax reveals the pattern, Praxis asks: what remains true when tested against experience, and what dissolves under contact with reality?

In development Enter

04

Axiom

Distills truth

The synthesis instrument. Axiom receives the outputs of the other three and distills them into durable principles, active tensions, and earned doctrines — the raw material of a personal constitution built from tested experience, not received wisdom.

The System

Not a sequence.
A cycle.

Each pass through the loop refines what came before. The light Lumen produces — the truths Axiom distills — becomes the next layer of darkness that Liminal interrogates. The cycle is the method.

01

Liminal

Questions belief

Surfaces hidden assumptions. Identifies what you believe that you have not chosen to believe.

02

Parallax

Reveals pattern

Reads across time. Finds recurring structures in thought, behavior, and emotional response.

03

Praxis

Tests hypothesis

Turns insight into experiment. Measures belief against lived experience before committing to it.

04

Axiom

Distills truth

Synthesizes what survived. Extracts durable principles, active tensions, and earned doctrines.

The truths distilled by Axiom become the assumptions interrogated by Liminal in the next cycle. The loop does not close — it deepens.

The Shell

A unified interface
for all four instruments.

The Lumen shell will serve as the parent environment — a single surface through which the four tools share state, communicate findings, and compound their outputs into a living personal constitution.

The examined life requires infrastructure. This is that infrastructure.
